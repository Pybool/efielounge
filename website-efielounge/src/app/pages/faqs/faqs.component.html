Why is the cost of courier not added to delivery charges?

There are so many factors that determine cost of delivery such as time and day when order is made. So, for example, if on a Sunday night, courier tend to charge more as there are less riders. It is as a result why we left this open. Rest assured, the price will be equivalent or less or equal to what obtains on Bolt Food, Uber or Hubtel.

Is Efie Lounge Kosher or Halal?

Efie Lounge is not a Halal or Kosher restaurant, as all our restaurants serve pork and beef and we do not have specific handling policies (including sourcing ingredients) that ensure the final dishes are Halal or Kosher.
